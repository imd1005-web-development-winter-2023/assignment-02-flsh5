Who is Mafumafu?

Mafumafu is a Japanese singer, song writer and composer. He is usually referred to as a Utaite, which is someone who covers Vocaloid songs and usually uses an anime version of themselves in their videos rather than their real identities. Did You Know?

On May 5th, 2021, Mafumafu rented one of the biggest stadiums in Japan to perform a virtual concert for free on Youtube!

In March 2020, Mafumafu had sold out shows to perform live at that stadium, however, because of the rapid spread of Covid-19, he had to cancel and refund all the tickets. A little over a year later, he decided to hold a show there again, only this time making it a worldwide distribution, and completely free! His reasoning was so that anyone from anywhere could join the stream and could hopefully get some joy during the troublesome times the world was going through. He has left the full stream available on Youtube, so people can always go back to it and enjoy it again.
